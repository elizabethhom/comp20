Homework Helpers is a private tutoring company that offers peer tutoring services to students in elementary, middle, high school students in Medford, Massachusetts. Homework Helpers was founded by a group of motivated, high-achieving Tufts University students, who have gone onto excell in many career paths, including engineering, medicine, and the social sciences.

Initially a team of only three students serving a handful of families, we have grown to encapsulate a team of over 50 tutors serving over 200 young students in the Medford area.

Interested in joining the team? We are always looking for new talent. Enter your email to updated about new job opportunities within the Homework Helper family.
